💡 What is UPI?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a digital payment system that allows you to send and receive money instantly using mobile apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm and BHIM.

  • Works 24×7 anytime
  • Needs only UPI ID or QR code
  • No need to remember bank account number
  • Transfers money instantly

⚙ How UPI Works

  • You enter receiver UPI ID
  • You confirm receiver name
  • You enter secure UPI PIN
  • Money transfers instantly

🔍 Real vs Fake Payment Request Comparison

✅ Real Payment

  • You are sending money
  • You enter your PIN yourself
  • Receiver name visible
  • App opens automatically

❌ Fake / Fraud Payment

  • Someone asks you to share OTP
  • Unknown payment request link
  • Prize / reward message
  • Urgent payment pressure

🛡 Important Safety Rules

  • Never share UPI PIN
  • Never share OTP
  • Always check receiver name
  • Avoid unknown payment links
  • Use trusted apps only

⚠ Example of Scam Message

"Congratulations! You won ₹5000 reward. Click this link and enter OTP to claim now."

This message is FAKE because UPI never asks OTP to receive money.

📷 Real vs Fake QR Code Example

Scammers sometimes place fake QR codes over original shop QR codes. Always verify before scanning.

✅ Real QR Code

  • Displayed by shop owner clearly
  • Shows correct shop name before payment
  • No sticker pasted over another QR
  • Inside trusted payment app

❌ Fake QR Code

  • Sticker pasted over original code
  • Unknown receiver name appears
  • Shared by strangers in WhatsApp
  • Promises reward or cashback

💡 Always check the receiver name before entering your UPI PIN.
